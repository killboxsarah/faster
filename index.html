TRUE TO THE ART

There is no perfect wheel or tire for everyone. Our wheels will not make you a better speed skater or a better derby player. However, every time you skate on faster wheels, you will reward all the hard work you have put in to your skating. You will actually feel the longer rolling, high rebounding urethane with dual-durometer pours maximizing your speed and edging. You will understand why you should be skating on beautifully engineered precision aluminium hubs for structural integrity and life long durability.

We strive to bring you the highest quality roller skate wheel choices because at the end of the day, we understand. We love to skate ourselves and know what it feels like to be on wheels that compliment your skills perfectly. We want to maximize your ability to get the best combination of grip, roll, responsiveness and control on any surface. Above all, well, we just want you to go faster.
